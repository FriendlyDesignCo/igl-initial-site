Vendor, Indie, & Artist Tables

A community gaming event wouldn't be complete without vendors selling their wares, indie studios demoing their latest games, and artists displaying their latest work. NOLA Gaming Fest will have 40+ designated tables available to rent, on a first-come, first-served basis. All tables are $60 and come with 1 full-access pass to NOLA Gaming Fest. holler@igl.gg
