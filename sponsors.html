Sponsors

While the Independent Gaming League is operated by a team of passionate gamers and scrappy event planners (in that order), it takes the extra effort and support of our invaluable sponsors to bring events like NOLA Gaming Fest to life. The companies below believed in IGL, NOLA Gaming Fest, and the community, so they were willing to step up and give their time, money, and attention in order to help us produce what is poised to be one of the most enjoyable community gaming events New Orleans has ever seen.

Thank you so very much to all of our sponsors, we absolutely could not have done this without you. If you'd like to get involved and be amongst this elite group of humans, just shoot us an email at holler@igl.gg
