Tournaments

Instead of recreating, reconfiguring, or repurposing an already-vibrant gaming community, we simply invited the area's best gaming event organizers to program a variety of tournaments for the games you love to play and compete in. To compete in any of these tournaments show up at NOLA Gaming Fest and give you entry fee to the tournament organizer. Entry fees for video games start at $5 and will not exceed $10 for any given tournament. If you have a question about a tournmanent contact the organizers on their facebook page.
