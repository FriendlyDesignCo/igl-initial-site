Tournaments

Instead of recreating, reconfiguring, or repurposing an already-vibrant gaming community, we simply invited the area's best gaming event organizers to program a variety of tournaments for the games you love to play and compete in. To compete in any of these tournaments, buy a ticket to NOLA Gaming Fest, show up and give your entry fee to the tournament organizer. Entry fees for video games start at $5 and will not exceed $10 for any given tournament. If you have a question about a specific tournament, contact the organizers on their respective Facebook page.
