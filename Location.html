Location & Travel

NOLA Gaming Fest will be located at the Pontchartrain Center (4545 Williams Blvd, Kenner, LA 70065), which is accessible via private automobile, taxi, or Uber. There is no charge for parking.

Hotel rooms are available at the Hilton Garden Inn New Orleans Airport, conveniently located directly next door to the Pontchartrain Center. Rooms are available for $119 per night and come equipped with two queen beds.

If you'd like to book a room, please call the Hilton Garden Inn at 504-712-0504. Our Group Name is: 'NOLA Gaming Fest' and our Group Code is: 'GAME'

If you have any difficulty booking a room or have any other question about traveling to our event, please email us at holler@igl.gg
