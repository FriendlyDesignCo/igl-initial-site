Lodging & Travel

NOLA Gaming Fest is located at the Pontchartrain Center (4545 Williams Blvd, Kenner, LA 70065) an is accesible via private automobile, taxi or Uber. There is no charge for parking.

Hotel rooms are available at the Hilton Garden Inn conveinently located directly next store to the Pontchartrian center. Rooms are availe to rent for $119 per night and come equiped with two queen beds. If you'd like to book a room please call the Hilton Garden Inn at 504-712-0504. Our Group Name is: 'NOLA Gaming Fest' and our Group Code is: 'GAME'

If you have any difficulty booking a room or have any other question about travelling to our event please email us at holler@igl.gg
