NOLA Gaming Fest will take place in New Orleans at the Pontchartrain Center on January 2nd and 3rd and will bring together casual gamers, pro-amateur gamers, game makers, and anyone else that considers themselves part of the gaming community.

At NOLA Gaming Fest, 5,000+ attendees will have the opportunity to learn, compete, and play in many ways, including structured tournaments for a variety of PC, console, and tabletop games. We will also be bringing in local indie game makers, artists, and vendors to keynote, participate in panels, and demo their games and products.
